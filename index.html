Welcome to OPAC's resource to understand, respond to, and prevent bullying. Together, we can create a safer and more supportive school environment. We are all in this together, and we can make a difference. We are all in this together, and we can make a difference.

At OPAC we belive that no student should be affected by bullying, which is why we have made this webiste to help students in Year 4 - 8 being bullied. Stopping Bullying can halt children and teenagers from negative thoughts, which can have an immense impact on their mental and physical behaviour as well as their wellbeing.

Bullying is a serious issue that can have long-lasting effects on students. It is essential to understand what bullying is and how to respond effectively. This website aims to provide valuable information and resources to address bullying in all its forms to students.
